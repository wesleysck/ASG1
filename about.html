About Us

 

Our start


Founded in 2015 by owner and head chef John Lim, Slices Pizza was started, with a goal of delivering fresh, quality pizza that's priced so affordable that you wouldn't have to think twice about ordering one!


We did it!


Since then, it's safe to say Slices has attained that goal, earning the Singapore Choice Awards for Local Foods in 2017 and serving happy customers daily. From just a single store in ION Orchard in 2015, Slices now has 5 outlets around Singapore.
